Add a sentiment to your mix

Way back in the day (the 70's), if you had something to say but didn't know how to say it, you would say it with a song. Thus the birth of the mixtape. A fun way to help your crush connect the dots, with Don't You by the Simple Minds. Or let your sister know you can't wait for her wedding with "I'm So Excited" by the Pointer Sisters. Fast Forward to the 21st century and the last couple years have been rough. A global pandemic has taken away the joys of human interaction. But one thing remained constant ... Music. And now we have the opportunity to once again just like they did back in the 70's
